Frequently asked questions

How much does bond cleaning cost in Bondi?

Prices vary depending on property size. A one-bedroom apartment typically costs $200–$280, while a three-bedroom house can range from $380–$550. Submit your details above for a free, tailored quote.

Do I get a bond-back guarantee?

Yes. Our cleaning partners offer a re-clean guarantee — if your property manager identifies any issues within a set period after the clean, the cleaner will return to rectify them at no additional cost.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3–5 days before your move-out date. Many of our cleaners offer same-week availability, but earlier bookings give you more flexibility.

Do I need to be present during the clean?

No. Most tenants leave a key or arrange access and return once the clean is complete. Your cleaner will let you know when they're done.

Is carpet steam cleaning included?

Carpet steam cleaning is usually an add-on service. Many leases require it, especially if carpets were professionally cleaned at the start of your tenancy. Your quote will include pricing for this if needed.
